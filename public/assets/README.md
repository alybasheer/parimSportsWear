# Assets Folder Structure

This folder is organized to help you easily add your business assets. Place your images in the appropriate folders:

## 📁 Folder Guide

### `/images/logos/`
- Your company logo (PNG with transparent background recommended)
- Suggested filename: `logo.png` or `logo-white.png` (for dark backgrounds)
- Recommended size: 500x500px or similar square format

### `/images/hero/`
- Hero/banner images for the homepage
- High-quality images of your sportswear products in action
- Recommended size: 1920x1080px (landscape)

### `/images/products/`
- Individual product photos
- Categories: jerseys, activewear, gym wear, custom uniforms, etc.
- Recommended: White background, high resolution (at least 1000x1000px)
- Name files descriptively: `jersey-blue-front.jpg`, `leggings-black.jpg`

### `/images/gallery/`
- Manufacturing facility photos
- Team photos
- Quality control process images
- Bulk order examples
- Recommended: Various sizes, professional quality

### `/images/team/`
- Team member photos (if you want an "Our Team" section)
- Recommended size: 500x500px square headshots

## 💡 Tips

1. **File Formats**: Use JPG for photos, PNG for logos/graphics with transparency
2. **File Names**: Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., `blue-jersey-front.jpg`)
3. **Optimization**: Compress images before uploading to improve website speed
4. **Consistency**: Try to maintain consistent lighting and backgrounds for product photos
